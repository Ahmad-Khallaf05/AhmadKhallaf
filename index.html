About

Turning Ideas into Interactive Digital Realities .

I'm Ahmad Khallaf, a Full Stack Web Developer with a solid background in both front-end and back-end development. I hold a degree in Artificial Intelligence from Aqaba University of Technology, where I developed a strong interest in building smart, user-centered web applications.

Over the past years, I’ve built and contributed to several web projects, including e-commerce platforms and task management systems, using technologies such as Laravel, React.js, HTML, and CSS. I completed a 3-month intensive training program at Orange Coding Academy, where I gained hands-on experience in full-stack development, agile teamwork, and project-based learning.

Currently, I’m working as a development intern at Lines of Innovation, where I continue to sharpen my skills and contribute to real-world web solutions. I’m passionate about transforming ideas into interactive digital realities—creating clean, responsive, and performance-driven websites that deliver results.
